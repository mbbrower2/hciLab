Current Focus

The current focus of our lab is on a new generation of "implicit" brain-computer interfaces. Our work in real-time measurement and machine learning classification of functional near infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS) brain data allows to create, use, and study new kinds of implicit user interfaces based on brain measurement.

Broader Focus

We are now also broadening this work to include other forms of lightweight, passive, real-time adaptive implicit interaction, based on physiological or other measurements. Our focus continues to be on the design of subtle and effective implicit interfaces that make judicious use of the measurements we can obtain..
