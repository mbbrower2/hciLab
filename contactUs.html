Undergraduate and MS Students

If you are interested in joining the lab, read this research paper, mention what you found the most intersting, and email it to hci@tufts.edu. Once you have done that join the general meeting and be prepared to meet with the PhD students afterwards, if you cannot make the general meeting include your availability for a short meeting in your email. The general lab meeting is at 12:00pm on Wednesdays in the HCI Lab, JCC room 362.

Students are expected to commit to at least one semester which will include weekly lab meetings and potentially additional 1:1 meetings with one of the PhD students. Members can do resarch on a volunteer basis, but once a relationship is established the potential for course credit is available.

PhD Students
